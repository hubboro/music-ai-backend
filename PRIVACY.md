# Privacy Notice

Butterfly is a small portfolio project that turns user prompts into shareable music soundtracks.

## What is stored

When you create or share a soundtrack, the backend may store:

- the prompt you typed
- the generated playlist name
- the generated song list
- the Spotify playlist URL, if a Spotify playlist is created
- basic operational metadata such as creation time and lightweight usage counters

The app also stores recent soundtracks in your browser's local storage so you can reopen them on the same device.

## Why it is stored

This data is stored to make share links work, let users reopen recent soundtracks, and keep basic operational visibility for the demo.

## What is not stored intentionally

The app is not designed to store passwords, payment information, or private Spotify account data. Spotify access tokens are only used to create playlists through the selected Spotify flow.

## Retention and deletion

Soundtrack records may remain in the database until they are manually removed. To request deletion of a soundtrack record, open an issue in this repository and include the share URL or soundtrack slug.

Do not put sensitive personal information in prompts.
